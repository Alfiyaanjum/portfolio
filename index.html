Eclipse-Java EE for backend support and Postman for testing API.My contibution in this project is of coding and designing.Objective and motive of project is to build a independent platform for needy as well as for generous people both can apply as per their requirement and help in the same way. Fundraising is essential to non-profit and charitable organizations. Unlike a for non-profit organization, non-profits do not generate sales revenue. The space between what a non-profit needs to serve the community and its base funding remains constant. Fundraising and social level goals do not just focus on filling that gap short-term. Finding and developing relationships among people with donors who will continue to make financial contributions is desirable. One of the ways non-profits accomplish this is by keeping track of donor information.

Chatbots is an Android based application that helps in instant reply with human. These are conversational bots that interact with you naturally. Chatbots can be mostly found on platforms like Facebook Messenger, Slack, WeChat, etc. Chatbots let users interact with intelligent solutions as though they are conversing with another person, and interactions can take many forms, from text/SMS to Office365 mail to Skype and Slack. From the users perspective, chatbots lets you get services from various service providers without downloading any app, which usually happens when you want to use any service from your smartphone. They can enhance your productivity in office, help you in buying tickets, paying bills, etc. There are various bot stores which act like app stores for chatbots. For example, Microsoft has bot directory, Slack has app directory, etc. From a developers perspective, bot developers can use bot frameworks and SDKs to build their bots for interaction, shopping, booking travel etc. These tools allow the bot developers to improve their bots by getting inputs from user, processing the input with help of some AI (artificial intelligence) and NLP (natural language processing), and then give user the services as per the bot's purpose of creation.
